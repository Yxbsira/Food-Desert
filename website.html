Food Deserts

Background


The Problem

Black communities suffer from lack of fresh produce in thier areas. Black communities are the ones targeted because of their social levels and lowerclass housing. This lack of fresh produce markets have made it easier than ever for people to get diabetes. Maany of this is due to fast food chains being found everywhere and the low cost prices of these meals. These food deserts are areas that dont have fresh produce markets in a one mile radius. A lot of these food deserts have a majority of black population than any other race. Many people in america have to make a far journey just for them to get groceries which no one shoudl go through and should be available for people around the nation. They need and deserve this food which should be recognized by the government and other bodies of government.
